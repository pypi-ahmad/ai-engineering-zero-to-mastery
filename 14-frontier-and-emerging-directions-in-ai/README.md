# Lesson 14: Frontier & Emerging Directions in AI

This lesson explores frontier AI directions that extend beyond standard production ML: neurosymbolic and causal reasoning, multi-agent complexity, quantum/neuromorphic paradigms, and long-term research contribution pathways.

## Sub-lessons

1. `14.1 Neurosymbolic AI & Causal Reasoning`
2. `14.2 Multi-Agent Systems & Complex Environments`
3. `14.3 Quantum & Neuromorphic AI (Conceptual Overview)`
4. `14.4 Lifelong Learning, Reading Groups & Contributing to the Field`

## How to use this lesson

1. Read each theory chapter for conceptual framing and terminology.
2. Run the notebooks to translate concepts into practical toy systems and planning templates.
3. Use the reading-group and roadmap sections to design your next learning cycle.
