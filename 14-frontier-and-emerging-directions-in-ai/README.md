# Lesson 14: Frontier & Emerging Directions in AI

This lesson explores frontier AI directions that extend beyond standard production ML: neurosymbolic and causal reasoning, multi-agent complexity, quantum/neuromorphic paradigms, long-term research contribution pathways, and GenAI observability/evaluation standards.

## Why This Matters

Frontier topics change quickly, but the skill is stable: reading critically, identifying assumptions, and connecting ideas to practical constraints (evaluation, safety, and deployment reality).

## Expected Outcomes

- You can read a new area and extract: problem, method, assumptions, and failure modes.
- You can decide whether a “frontier” approach is relevant to a real system you could build.

1. Read each theory chapter for conceptual framing and terminology.
2. Run the notebooks to translate concepts into practical toy systems and planning templates.
3. Use 14.5 to align your telemetry and evaluation practices with emerging GenAI standards.
4. Use the reading-group and roadmap sections to design your next learning cycle.
