# Lesson 11: AI Product Management, Entrepreneurship & Research Methods

This lesson bridges AI engineering with product strategy, venture design, and research rigor. It is designed for builders who want to ship AI products, evaluate business viability, and execute research-quality experimentation.

## Why This Matters

You can build impressive models and still fail to deliver impact. Product framing and research rigor help you choose the right problems, validate value, and communicate tradeoffs.

## Expected Outcomes

- You can write a clear problem statement with constraints and success metrics.
- You can design an experiment plan (baseline, evaluation, iteration loop).
- You can produce a portfolio narrative that explains what you built and why it matters.

## How to Use This Lesson

1. Start with each chapter in `theory/` for conceptual grounding.
2. Use notebook templates to practice PM, startup, and research workflows.
3. Reuse capstone and portfolio artifacts for interviews, applications, and launches.

## Verify Your Work

- Write one “PRD-lite” (one page): users, problem, constraints, success metrics.
- Define a small experiment plan: baseline -> eval -> iteration -> ship gate.
