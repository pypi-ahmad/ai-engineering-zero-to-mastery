# 15.3 Teamwork, Communication & Stakeholder Management

Covers the collaboration and communication layer of capstone success: role clarity, decision logs, expectation setting, and stakeholder reporting.

## Start Here

1. Theory: `theory/15-3-teamwork-communication-and-stakeholder-management.md`

## Outcomes

- Use concrete templates for weekly updates, meeting notes, and decisions.
- Handle scope conflicts and misalignment with structured responses.
- Communicate technical work effectively to non-technical stakeholders.

## Includes

- communication scoring rubric for team execution quality,
- team reflection template for post-capstone process improvement,
- stakeholder-update and escalation playbooks.
